I was born in Chennai, India and spent my first 8 years there before my family immigrated to the United States. So, we moved from India to a smaller version of it - New Jersey. I grew up there for most of my life and then moved to Dallas right in the middle of COVID in 2020 because well there's nothing quite like moving in the middle of a global pandemic.

Following the move, I finished my Bachelor's in Biology with a Psychology minor in 2024. And instead of heading off to med school, I pulled a complete 180 and went straight into my Master's in Business Analytics and AI, graduating this past December 2025. Currently, I'm working at NCR Atleos as a Product Analyst, sitting at the intersection of finance, business, and AI.

So yeah, you're probably wondering: what happened to becoming a doctor? Stick around and check out my blog to find out!

## What I'm into

With that, you've got a pretty good picture of how I spend my free time.
